Cairo Force — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

This privacy policy applies to the mobile game Cairo Force (the "App"), developed and published by salfedev (Sal Abuewilly) ("we", "us"), and distributed via Google Play. It explains what data the App handles, how, and your choices.

1. Summary

  • Cairo Force is playable fully offline. The core game does not require an account and does not collect personal data.
  • Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device only.
  • The optional online leaderboard is opt-in and uses Google Firebase (see section 3).
  • We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with third parties except the service providers listed below.

2. Data stored on your device

The App saves your game progress (campaign clears, stars, unlocks, scores), gameplay settings, and tuning preferences in a local save file on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you use the optional online features described below. Uninstalling the App deletes it.

3. Optional online leaderboard (Firebase)

If you choose to use the Endless-mode online leaderboard, the App uses Google Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore) to store and display:

  • an anonymous account identifier (or your Google account identity, only if you explicitly sign in with Google);
  • the display name you choose for the leaderboard;
  • your best Endless wave score and the time it was submitted.

This data is used solely to operate the leaderboard. Firebase is operated by Google LLC; see the Firebase Privacy and Security documentation and Google's Privacy Policy. If you never open the leaderboard or never submit a score, no online data is created.

4. Data we do not collect

  • No location data.
  • No contacts, photos, files, microphone, or camera access.
  • No advertising identifiers (the current version of the App contains no advertising).
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs in the current version.

Future versions of the App are planned to include optional rewarded advertising, in-app purchases, and privacy-preserving gameplay analytics. If and when those features ship, this policy will be updated before release, the "Last updated" date will change, and any legally required consent (for example GDPR consent in the EEA/UK) will be requested in-app before any ad or analytics data is processed.

5. Data retention and deletion

  • Local save data: retained on your device until you delete it (in-game "New Game" or uninstalling the App).
  • Leaderboard data: retained while the leaderboard service operates. You can request deletion of your leaderboard entry and associated account identifier at any time by emailing us (below); we will remove it within 30 days.

6. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal data via the leaderboard, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Security

Leaderboard data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and stored in Google Firebase with access rules restricting writes to authenticated sessions. No payment or password data is handled by the App.

8. Your rights

Depending on your region (including the EEA and UK under GDPR), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. The only personal data we may hold is the leaderboard data described in section 3 — contact us to exercise these rights.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the App evolves (for example when advertising or in-app purchases are added). Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date before they take effect.
